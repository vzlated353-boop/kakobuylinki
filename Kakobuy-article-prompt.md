## Content Requirements

### 1. Keyword Usage
- **Primary keyword**: Must appear in:
  - H1 title
  - First paragraph (within first 100 words)
  - At least one H2 heading
  - Conclusion paragraph
  - Total 3-5 times throughout the article
- **Keyword density**: 1.5%-2.5%
- **LSI keywords**: Include 5-8 related terms naturally

### 2. Data Points (Must Include)
Every article must include at least 3 of these data types:
- Product count: "22,000+ verified products"
- Price range: "$14.99-$139.99"
- Order counts: "410+ orders"
- Quality pass rates: "94% quality pass rate"
- Satisfaction rates: "93% buyer satisfaction"
- Shipping times: "10-14 days to US/EU"
- Repurchase rates: "72% of buyers reorder"

### 3. Internal Links
- **In-text links**: 2-3 links within the main content
- **Related Guides section**: 3 links at the end
- **Anchor text**: Must include the target page's keyword
- **Link format**: `[Anchor text with keyword](relative-path-to-file.md)`

### 4. E-E-A-T Compliance
- **Experience**: Mention real order data, user feedback, community insights
- **Expertise**: Explain technical details accurately (QC process, batch versions, sizing)
- **Authoritativeness**: Cite community size, order volume, platform reputation
- **Trustworthiness**: Acknowledge limitations, set realistic expectations, avoid absolute claims

### 5. Tone and Style
- **Professional**: Data-driven, informative
- **Buyer-focused**: Address reader needs and concerns
- **Conversational**: Use "you" and "we" naturally
- **Confident**: Assertive but not arrogant

### 6. Product Examples
For category/brand pages, include 3-5 specific products:
```
- **Product Name**: $Price | X+ orders | Y% quality pass rate | Brief description
```

For feature pages, include relevant examples that demonstrate the feature.

### 7. FAQ Questions
Choose from these common question types:
- "What is [keyword]?"
- "How does [keyword] work?"
- "Is [keyword] legit/safe?"
- "How much does [keyword] cost?"
- "How long does [keyword] take?"
- "What are the best [keyword] products?"
- "How to use [keyword]?"
- "Where to find [keyword]?"

### 8. Conclusion Elements
- Summarize 2-3 key benefits
- Reinforce Kakobuy's value proposition
- Include a clear call to action
- Mention the spreadsheet one final time

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing each article, verify:

- [ ] H1 title includes primary keyword
- [ ] Keyword appears in first 100 words
- [ ] Keyword appears 3-5 times total
- [ ] At least 3 data points included (prices, order counts, quality rates)
- [ ] 3-5 specific product examples with prices
- [ ] 2-3 internal links in main content
- [ ] 3 internal links in Related Guides section
- [ ] FAQ section has 3-5 questions
- [ ] Conclusion includes call to action
- [ ] All content is original (not copied from existing articles)
- [ ] Tone is professional and buyer-focused
- [ ] E-E-A-T elements are present (experience, expertise, authority, trust)

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

—?**Keyword stuffing**: Don't force keyword unnaturally
—?**Generic content**: Don't write vague statements without data
—?**Missing data points**: Every article needs specific numbers
—?**No internal links**: Must include 5+ total internal links
—?**Copying existing articles**: All content must be 100% original
—?**Ignoring E-E-A-T**: Must demonstrate experience, expertise, authority, trust
—?**Absolute claims**: Avoid "always," "never," "guaranteed" —?use "typically," "usually," "most"
