## Quality Verification for Dior B30 Sneakers

The Dior B30's technical construction creates specific quality checkpoints that differ from both traditional luxury sneakers and performance running shoes. Thorough QC photo review is essential for ensuring you receive a product that meets expectations.

**Mesh Panel Quality**

The mesh panels are the most visible element of the B30's upper, and their quality is critical to both appearance and durability. In QC photos, examine the mesh weave for consistency ??it should be evenly tensioned with uniform hole size and no snags or pulls. The mesh should lie flat against the foot without wrinkles or sagging. Check that the mesh is securely attached to the underlying layers and nylon overlays ??there should be no gaps or loose areas where the mesh appears about to separate. The mesh should have the correct density ??not so open that it looks cheap or flimsy, but not so dense that it compromises breathability.

**Nylon Overlay Construction**

The nylon overlays on the B30 serve both structural and aesthetic functions, and their construction quality is a key indicator of overall batch quality. Check that the nylon panels are cleanly cut with straight edges and no fraying. The overlays should be evenly tensioned across their surface, with no wrinkles, bubbles, or areas where the material appears stretched or distorted. Stitching along the edges of the nylon panels should be even and consistent, with no loose threads or skipped stitches. Where nylon overlays intersect or overlap, the seams should be clean and well-finished. The nylon material should have the correct semi-matte sheen ??not too glossy, not too flat.

**Sole Unit Inspection**

The sole unit on the Dior B30 should be a well-finished piece of rubber with no defects. Check that the sole is properly bonded to the upper with no visible gaps, excess adhesive, or misalignment. The tread pattern should be accurately reproduced with proper depth and definition ??shallow or poorly defined tread indicates inferior molding quality. The edges of the sole should be cleanly finished with no rough spots or flash marks from the molding process. The midsole should have consistent thickness and density, with no areas that appear compressed or uneven. Any issues with sole bonding or tread quality are grounds for a return request.

**Branding Accuracy**

Dior branding on the B30 should be precise and properly positioned. The lateral logo patch should be centered, properly sized, and cleanly attached with no visible adhesive bleeding around the edges. The text should be crisp with accurate Dior font reproduction. The tongue tag should have clean edges and accurate text with proper stitching attachment. The heel tab branding should be centered and legible. Check that all branding elements are consistent between the left and right shoe in terms of size, position, and execution. Small details like these are where lower-quality batches often fail.

The Dior B30 generally runs true to European size for most buyers with standard-width feet. **Narrow Feet Considerations**

Buyers with narrower feet may find the B30 slightly roomy in the midfoot area due to its running shoe-inspired design. Buyers with wider feet should stick with their regular European size or consider going up half a size if they are between sizes. **How do I clean Dior B30 sneakers without damaging them?**
