When reviewing QC photos for Jordan shorts, focus on these critical quality indicators:

**Fabric Quality and Texture**: The fabric is the most important factor in shorts quality. High-quality mesh shorts should feature the distinctive diamond pattern with proper breathability and durability. The mesh should feel substantial, not flimsy, and should maintain its shape when stretched. Nylon shorts should have a smooth, water-resistant finish with consistent color and no visible defects. Cotton-blend shorts should feel soft and comfortable against the skin, with even weaving and no loose threads. Compare the QC photos against reference images of retail Jordan shorts to assess the fabric quality.

**Stitching and Construction**: Examine the stitching along the waistband, leg openings, side seams, and pockets. Quality Jordan shorts feature even, tight stitching with no loose threads or uneven spacing. The waistband should be securely attached with reinforced stitching at stress points. The side seams should be straight and symmetrical, with no puckering or gaps. Pay attention to the connection points where the waistband meets the shorts body, as these areas often reveal quality differences.

**Logo and Branding Accuracy**: The Jumpman logo should be properly positioned, proportioned, and clearly defined. On mesh shorts, the logo is typically embroidered on the left leg. On nylon shorts, it may be embroidered or printed. Check that the logo matches reference images in terms of size, placement, and color. The embroidery should be clean with no loose threads, and printed logos should have sharp edges without bleeding or fading.

**Sizing Considerations**: Jordan shorts typically come in sizes ranging from S to XXL, with waist measurements corresponding to standard sizing. However, fit can vary between batches and styles.
