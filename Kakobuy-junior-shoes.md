## Understanding Junior Sizing

Junior sizing can be confusing because it sits between children's and adult sizes, and different brands handle this transition differently. In the Kakobuy junior shoes collection, sizes typically range from youth 5 to youth 9, which corresponds roughly to foot lengths of 9.25 inches to 10.25 inches (23.5 cm to 26 cm). However, the exact fit can vary by brand and model, making it essential to consult the specific sizing information provided in the spreadsheet rather than relying solely on the labeled size.

Nike junior shoes generally run true to size but have a slightly narrower fit compared to adult versions. This means that if your teen has wider feet, you might want to size up by half a size. Adidas junior models tend to have a more accommodating toe box, making them suitable for teens with wider feet without needing to size up. The Jordan 4 in junior sizes maintains the same proportions as adult versions but scaled down, so if your teen is between sizes, sizing up is usually the safer choice. The spreadsheet includes detailed measurements and fit notes for each model, helping you make more accurate selections.

The transition from kids' sizes to junior sizes typically happens around age 10-12, though this varies significantly based on individual growth patterns. Similarly, the move from junior to adult sizes usually occurs between ages 14-16. It's important to note that junior sizes aren't just smaller versions of adult shoes—they're designed with different proportions that account for the way teens' feet are still developing. This means better support in key areas and construction that accommodates growth patterns.

When measuring your teen's feet, do so in the afternoon when feet are naturally slightly larger from daily activity. Have them stand on a piece of paper with their heel against a wall, mark the longest toe, and measure the distance in centimeters. Compare this to the sizing chart in the spreadsheet, adding about 0.5 cm for growth room if they're still growing rapidly. For teens whose growth has stabilized, you can fit more precisely without extra room. Checking recent buyer feedback in the community forums can also reveal how specific models fit in practice, giving you additional confidence in your size selection.
