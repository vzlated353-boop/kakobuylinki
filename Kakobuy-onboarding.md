## Understanding Quality Verification and Batch Versions

Quality verification is the cornerstone of the Kakobuy platform. Before any item ships to you, it passes through a QC (Quality Control) checkpoint where trained inspectors photograph the product from multiple angles, verify materials and construction, and compare it against reference images. This process ensures that what you ordered is what you receive.

The QC photo system is transparent and accessible. Once your order is processed, you receive QC photos via your dashboard. These photos show the actual item — front, back, sides, close-ups of logos, stitching, and materials. You can review them and either approve the item for shipping or request changes if something does not match your expectations.

Batch versions are an important concept in this market. The same product may be produced in different batches, and there can be slight variations in materials, color accuracy, or construction quality between batches. Kakobuy clearly labels batch versions in the spreadsheet, allowing you to choose based on your priorities. Some buyers prefer the cheapest option, while others pay slightly more for the highest-quality batch.

The quality pass rate system provides historical context for each product. This rate represents the percentage of items from that batch that passed QC inspection. For example, if a product has a 95% quality pass rate, it means 95 out of 100 items from that batch met quality standards. Higher rates indicate more consistent quality and lower risk of receiving a defective item.

For detailed guidance on interpreting QC photos and understanding batch differences, the [Kakobuy dashboard guide](/Kakobuy-dashboard-guide/) provides visual examples and best practices. This knowledge helps you make smarter choices and set accurate expectations for your purchases.

## Placing Your First Order

Once you have selected your product and understand the quality data, placing your first order is straightforward. Click "Add to Cart" from the product listing, then proceed to checkout. At checkout, you will confirm your size, color, and shipping address. If you saved this information in your profile, it auto-fills for convenience.

Choose your shipping method based on your timeline and budget. Kakobuy offers three primary options:

- **Standard Shipping**: 12-18 days transit, most economical, suitable for non-urgent orders
- **Express Shipping**: 7-10 days transit, moderate cost, good balance of speed and value
- **Priority Shipping**: 5-7 days transit, premium pricing, for time-sensitive purchases

For first-time buyers, Standard Shipping is usually sufficient. You can always upgrade for future orders once you are comfortable with the process. Delivery to the US and EU typically takes 10-14 days total with standard shipping, including processing and QC time.

Select your payment method — Kakobuy accepts cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum), wire transfer, and popular digital payment services. All transactions are processed through encrypted gateways for security. Once payment is confirmed, your order enters the processing queue.

Most orders process within 2-3 business days. During this time, your item is sourced, inspected, and photographed for QC. You receive notifications at each stage via email and dashboard alerts. The support team is available if you have questions or need updates on your order status.

## FAQ

### Q1: How long does the Kakobuy onboarding process take?
A: Account creation takes less than five minutes. Exploring the catalog and understanding the quality system may take 15-30 minutes for first-time users. Placing your first order adds another 5-10 minutes. In total, you can complete onboarding and place your first order within an hour.

### Q2: Do I need to verify my identity to use Kakobuy?
A: No, Kakobuy does not require identity verification for standard purchases. You only need a valid email address and secure password to create an account. Identity verification may be required for certain payment methods or high-value orders, but this is handled on a case-by-case basis.

### Q3: What happens after I place my first order?
A: After payment, your order enters processing. Within 2-3 business days, you receive QC photos via your dashboard. You review and approve the photos, then the item ships. You receive a tracking number and can monitor delivery progress. Standard shipping to the US and EU takes 10-14 days total.

### Q4: Can I contact support if I have questions during onboarding?
A: Yes, Kakobuy provides customer support through the dashboard ticket system. The support team typically responds within 24 hours. You can also access FAQs, sizing guides, and other resources through the platform. For urgent issues, live chat may be available during business hours.
