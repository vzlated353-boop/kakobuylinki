## Evaluating Seller Credentials

Before making any purchase, thoroughly examine the seller's profile metrics. Start with their overall rating, which should be 4.7 stars or higher for reliable performance. ## Communication Best Practices

Effective seller communication starts with clear, specific inquiries. When asking about product availability, include exact model numbers, colorways, and size requirements. Vague questions receive vague answers, while precise requests enable accurate responses. Top-rated sellers appreciate detailed inquiries because they reduce back-and-forth exchanges.

Response time expectations should align with seller ratings. Premium sellers respond within 4 hours during business hours, while acceptable sellers respond within 24 hours. If a seller consistently takes longer than 48 hours to respond, consider this a red flag indicating potential service issues.

When discussing pricing, be respectful and realistic. Sellers operate with thin margins, and aggressive haggling often backfires. Instead, ask about bulk discounts, seasonal promotions, or bundle deals. Many sellers offer 5-10% discounts for orders exceeding three items, which provides legitimate savings without damaging the relationship.

Document all agreements in the platform's messaging system. This creates a record that protects both parties if disputes arise. Clear communication about expectations, timelines, and quality standards prevents most common issues before they occur.

## Quality Control and Inspection

Understanding the quality control process helps you evaluate seller reliability. Reputable sellers provide detailed QC photos showing products from multiple angles before shipping. These photos reveal color accuracy, material quality, stitching details, and overall construction. Compare QC photos against reference images to identify discrepancies.

Quality pass rates of 92-95% indicate sellers who consistently deliver products matching their descriptions. Sellers below 88% should be avoided, as this suggests systematic quality issues. Pay attention to specific complaints in reviews mentioning color variation, sizing inaccuracy, or material quality problems.

Request additional photos if standard QC images don't address your concerns. Most quality sellers accommodate reasonable requests for specific angle shots or detail close-ups. This willingness to provide additional documentation demonstrates confidence in product quality and commitment to customer satisfaction.

## FAQ

**How do I find reliable sellers on Kakobuy?**
Start by filtering for sellers with 4.7+ star ratings, 92%+ quality pass rates, and 300+ monthly orders. Cross-reference these metrics with recent reviews and QC photo quality to identify trustworthy sellers.

**What should I ask sellers before purchasing?**
