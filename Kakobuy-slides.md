## FAQ

### Q1: What is the difference between EVA foam and cushioned foam slides?
A: EVA foam is lightweight, water-resistant, and durable, making it perfect for poolside, beach, and post-workout wear. It's the most common material for athletic slides. Cushioned foam provides premium comfort with a memory foam-like feel, ideal for extended wear around the house or casual outings. Cushioned foam is typically found in higher-priced slides and offers better arch support.

### Q2: Do Kakobuy slides run true to size?
A: Most slides run slightly small due to the open-toe design and strap placement. We recommend going half a size up for optimal comfort, especially if you plan to wear socks. Check the size chart provided in product photos and compare with your measurements. Read buyer reviews for specific fit feedback on each style, as some designs may run larger or smaller than others.

### Q3: Can I wear slides for athletic activities?
A: Yes, athletic slides are designed for post-workout recovery and casual sports wear. They provide comfort and breathability after intense activity, helping feet recover. However, slides don't offer the support needed for running or high-impact sports. For actual athletic performance, choose proper [Kakobuy running shoes](/Kakobuy-running-shoes/) or training footwear. Slides are best for warm-up, cool-down, and recovery periods.

### Q4: How do I clean and maintain my slides?
A: Cleaning depends on the material. EVA foam slides can be rinsed with water and mild soap, then air dried. Avoid direct sunlight or heat sources, which can warp the material. Cushioned foam slides should be spot cleaned with a damp cloth and allowed to air dry. For luxury-inspired slip-ons with fabric straps, use a soft brush and appropriate cleaner for the material. Regular cleaning extends slide life and maintains appearance.
