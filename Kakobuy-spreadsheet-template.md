## Template Structure and Customization

The Kakobuy spreadsheet template includes several key components that you can customize to match your needs:

You can add or remove columns based on your priorities.

**Comparison Matrix**: A dedicated comparison sheet allows you to evaluate similar products across multiple criteria. You can weight different factors (like quality, price, and features) based on their importance to you, and the template calculates a weighted score for each product.

**Price Tracking Log**: This sheet helps you monitor price changes over time for products you're considering. You can log prices at different dates and use built-in formulas to calculate price trends and identify the best times to buy.

**Quality Metrics Dashboard**: A summary dashboard provides visual indicators for the quality metrics of your tracked products. Color-coded cells highlight products with excellent, good, acceptable, or risky quality ratings, making it easy to assess your options at a glance.

**Wishlist and Priority Ranking**: A wishlist sheet allows you to organize products you're interested in but not ready to purchase yet. You can rank items by priority, set target prices, and track when items go on sale.

**Custom Fields**: The template includes space for custom fields that reflect your specific needs. You can add columns for shipping time preferences, color options, size requirements, or any other criteria that matter to you.
