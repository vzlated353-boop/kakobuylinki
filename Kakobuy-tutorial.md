## Tutorial Step 1: Account Setup and Dashboard Orientation

**Action**: Open your browser and navigate to the Kakobuy homepage. Click the "Register" button in the top right corner.

**What happens**: A registration form appears requesting your email address, password, and basic information. Fill in all required fields and click "Submit." You will receive a verification email within minutes.

**Next step**: Open your email inbox, find the verification message from Kakobuy, and click the activation link. This confirms your email and activates your account. Return to the Kakobuy homepage and log in with your credentials.

**Dashboard overview**: After logging in, you land on the main dashboard. Here is what you see:

- **Top navigation bar**: Search field, notification bell, account menu, and cart icon
- **Left sidebar**: Product catalog, order history, shipping calculator, support tickets, and account settings
- **Main panel**: Active orders summary, recent QC photos awaiting review, shipping status updates, and account balance
- **Quick stats**: Total orders placed, items in transit, and pending QC reviews

Take two minutes to click through each sidebar menu item. This familiarizes you with the layout and helps you navigate efficiently later. The [Kakobuy how to use](/Kakobuy-how-to-use/) guide covers each section in more detail, but this orientation gives you the mental map you need.

**Pro tip**: Complete your profile now by adding your shipping address and preferred sizes. This saves time during checkout and ensures accuracy on your first order.

## Tutorial Step 2: Searching and Browsing Products

**Action**: Click on "Product Catalog" in the left sidebar. The catalog opens showing five main categories: Clothing, Shoes, Pants, Accessories, and Watches.

**What happens**: Each category displays subcategories with product counts. For example, clicking "Clothing" reveals T-shirts (44), Hoodies (19), Tracksuits (21), Down Jackets (9), and more. Click any subcategory to see individual product listings.

**Using search**: The search bar at the top accepts keywords, brand names, or product types. Try searching "Nike" to see all Nike products across categories. Try "hoodie" to see all hoodies regardless of brand. The search supports partial matches and returns results instantly.

**Filtering results**: Use the filter panel on the left side of search results to narrow by:
- Price range (set minimum and maximum)
- Brand (select from dropdown)
- Category (Clothing, Shoes, Pants, Accessories, Watches)
- Availability (in stock, coming soon, pre-order)

**Reading product listings**: Each listing shows:
- Product image and name
- Approximate price in USD
- Quality pass rate with color-coded badge (green for 94%+, yellow for 90-94%, orange for below 90%)
- Order count indicating popularity
- Available sizes and colors

Click any product to open its detail page, which includes expanded photos, batch version information, sizing charts, and customer reviews.

**Pro tip**: Use the comparison tool to save up to five products and view them side by side. This is especially useful when choosing between batch versions of the same item.

## Tutorial Step 3: Understanding Quality Data and the Spreadsheet

**Action**: On any product detail page, scroll to the "Quality Information" section. Here you will find batch version details, quality pass rates, and historical data.

**What the data means**:
- **Batch version**: Identifies the production batch. Different batches may have slight variations in materials or construction.
- **Quality pass rate**: The percentage of items from this batch that passed QC inspection. A 95% rate means 95 out of 100 items met quality standards.
- **Order count**: Total units sold. Higher counts indicate proven reliability and consistent quality.

## Tutorial Step 4: Placing Your Order

**Action**: From the product detail page, select your size and color, then click "Add to Cart." The cart icon in the top navigation updates to show the item count.

**Checkout process**: Click the cart icon to review your selections. Verify the product, size, color, and quantity. Click "Proceed to Checkout."

**Shipping selection**: Choose your shipping method:
- Standard Shipping (12-18 days) ˇŞ most economical
- Express Shipping (7-10 days) ˇŞ moderate cost
- Priority Shipping (5-7 days) ˇŞ premium pricing

For first orders, Standard Shipping is recommended. Delivery to the US and EU typically takes 10-14 days total including processing time.

**Payment**: Select your payment method. Kakobuy accepts cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum), wire transfer, and digital payment services. Enter the required information and confirm payment. All transactions use encrypted gateways for security.

**Order confirmation**: After payment, you receive an order confirmation via email and dashboard notification. Your order enters the processing queue. Typical processing time is 2-3 business days.

**Pro tip**: If you are ordering multiple items, consider using the consolidation service. Items can be combined into a single shipment, reducing total shipping costs.

## Tutorial Step 5: QC Review and Shipping

**Action**: Wait for the QC photo notification. This typically arrives within 2-3 business days after payment confirmation.

**Reviewing QC photos**: Open your dashboard and navigate to "Pending QC Reviews." You will see photos of your actual item ˇŞ front, back, sides, close-ups of logos, stitching, and materials. Compare these photos against the product listing images.

**Decision time**:
- **Approve**: If the item matches your expectations, click "Approve." The item proceeds to packaging and shipping.
- **Request changes**: If you notice issues ˇŞ wrong color, visible defects, or significant differences ˇŞ click "Request Replacement" or "Request Additional Photos." The support team responds within 24 hours.

**Shipping and tracking**: Once approved, your item ships within 1-2 business days. You receive a tracking number via email and dashboard notification. Track your package in real-time through the dashboard's shipping panel.

**Delivery**: Standard shipping to the US and EU takes 10-14 days total. The package arrives in discreet packaging with no external branding. Inspect the item upon delivery and confirm everything matches the QC photos.

**Post-delivery**: If there are any issues, you have a 7-day window to report them through the support ticket system. Resolution rates for legitimate claims exceed 95%.

## FAQ

### Q1: How long does the entire Kakobuy tutorial process take from start to finish?
A: Account setup takes about 5 minutes. Browsing and selecting products typically takes 15-30 minutes. Checkout and payment require another 5-10 minutes. QC review happens 2-3 days later, and delivery takes 10-14 days. Total time from registration to receiving your package is approximately two to three weeks.

### Q2: What should I check in the QC photos?
A: Examine the overall color accuracy, logo placement and quality, stitching consistency, material texture, and any visible defects. Compare the QC photos against the product listing images. If anything looks significantly different, request additional photos or a replacement before approving.

### Q3: Can I change my order after placing it?
A: Changes can be made before QC processing begins. Once your item has been inspected and photographed, changes become more difficult. If you need to modify your order, contact support immediately through the dashboard ticket system.

### Q4: What happens if my package is lost or damaged during shipping?
A: Kakobuy provides tracking for all shipments. If your package shows as delivered but you have not received it, contact support within 7 days. For damaged packages, photograph the damage and submit a ticket with evidence. Resolution rates for legitimate claims exceed 95%.
