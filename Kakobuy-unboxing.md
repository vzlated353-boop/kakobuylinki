## Step-by-Step Unboxing Process

The unboxing process should be methodical to ensure you don't damage your items or lose important documentation. Start by examining the exterior of the package for any visible damage such as tears, crushing, or water stains. If the package appears damaged, take photos before opening it, as this documentation may be needed for insurance claims or replacement requests. Most Kakobuy packages arrive in excellent condition, but it's always wise to document any issues immediately.

Next, carefully open the package using scissors or a box cutter. Cut along the tape seams rather than through the box itself to avoid accidentally damaging the contents. For poly mailers, tear along the sealed edge or use scissors to cut it open. Inside, you'll find your items wrapped in protective materials such as bubble wrap, tissue paper, or poly bags. Remove these layers gently to reveal your products.

For clothing items, you'll typically find garments folded and placed in individual poly bags to protect against moisture and dirt. Unfold each item and check for any wrinkles, stains, or damage. Most clothing items like the Essentials T-shirt (~$9.73) or Trapstar Hoodie (~$26.04) arrive in excellent condition, but it's important to inspect them thoroughly while you still have the option to request exchanges or returns if needed.

Footwear arrives in original retail boxes, which are then wrapped in protective film or placed inside a larger shipping box. Open the shoe box carefully and remove the tissue paper stuffing that helps maintain the shoe's shape. Inspect the sneakers for any defects, checking the stitching, sole attachment, and overall finish. The Nike Air Max 95 (~$46.15) and Dior B22 (~$45.33) typically arrive in perfect condition, but a thorough inspection ensures you're satisfied with your purchase.

Accessories and small items like phone cases (~$1.30), jewelry (~$1.68 to $25.00), or watches are usually placed in small boxes or padded envelopes. Remove the items carefully and check for any damage. Watches in particular should be inspected for scratches, dial alignment, and strap condition. The platform's 94% quality pass rate means most items meet high standards, but personal inspection gives you peace of mind.
