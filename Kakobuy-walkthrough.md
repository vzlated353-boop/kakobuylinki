## Stage 1: First Visit and Account Creation

**What you see**: You arrive at the Kakobuy homepage. The page displays a clean, modern interface with the platform logo at the top, a search bar prominently positioned, and navigation menus for product categories. Below the header, you see featured products, popular items, and promotional banners highlighting current deals or new arrivals.

**Visual elements**:
- Top navigation bar: Search field, notification bell, account/login button, cart icon
- Left sidebar: Product categories (Clothing, Shoes, Pants, Accessories, Watches)
- Main content area: Featured products grid with images, prices, and quality badges
- Footer: Links to support, FAQs, terms of service, and contact information

**Your action**: Click the "Register" button in the top right corner. A registration form slides into view requesting your email address, password, and basic information. Fill in the fields and click "Submit."

**What happens next**: The form submits and you see a confirmation message: "Registration successful! Please check your email to verify your account." Open your email inbox and locate the verification message from Kakobuy. Click the activation link inside.

**Visual confirmation**: After clicking the verification link, your browser redirects back to Kakobuy with a success message: "Account verified! You can now log in." Enter your credentials on the login page and access your dashboard.

**Dashboard first impression**: The dashboard loads showing your account overview. You see:
- Welcome message with your email address
- Quick stats panel: 0 orders, 0 items in transit, 0 pending QC reviews
- Navigation sidebar ready for you to explore the product catalog
- Notification area (currently empty, but will populate as you use the platform)

This is your starting point. The dashboard is your command center for all future activities on Kakobuy.

## Stage 3: Checkout and Payment

**What you see**: Click the cart icon in the top navigation. Your cart page loads showing the item you added — product image, name, selected size and color, quantity, and price. Below the item, you see order summary: subtotal, shipping cost (calculated after you select shipping method), and total.

**Checkout flow**: Click "Proceed to Checkout." The checkout page loads with three sections:

**Section 1 — Shipping Address**: If you saved an address in your profile, it auto-fills here. Otherwise, enter your shipping address including name, street address, city, state/province, postal code, country, and phone number. Double-check for accuracy — errors here can delay delivery.

**Section 2 — Shipping Method**: Three radio button options appear:
- Standard Shipping (12-18 days) — $X.XX (most economical)
- Express Shipping (7-10 days) — $X.XX (moderate cost)
- Priority Shipping (5-7 days) — $X.XX (premium pricing)

Select your preferred method. For first orders, Standard Shipping is usually sufficient. The shipping cost updates in the order summary automatically.

**Section 3 — Payment Method**: Select from available payment options:
- Cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum)
- Wire Transfer
- Digital Payment Services

Choose your method and enter the required information. For cryptocurrency, you receive a wallet address to send payment. For wire transfer, you receive bank details. For digital payments, you are redirected to the payment processor.

**Order review**: Before finalizing, review your complete order:
- Items: Product name, size, color, quantity, price
- Shipping address: Complete address details
- Shipping method: Selected option with delivery timeline
- Payment method: Selected option
- Order total: Subtotal + shipping + any applicable fees

**Visual confirmation**: Click "Place Order." A confirmation page loads showing your order number, estimated delivery date, and next steps. You also receive an email confirmation with the same information.

**What happens next**: Your order enters the processing queue. You see a status indicator on your dashboard: "Processing — Awaiting QC Inspection."

## Stage 4: Quality Verification and QC Photo Review

**What you see**: Two to three business days after payment, you receive a notification: "QC Photos Ready for Review." Click the notification or navigate to "Pending QC Reviews" in your dashboard.

**QC photo gallery**: The review page displays multiple photos of your actual item:
- Front view
- Back view
- Side views (left and right)
- Close-up of logo or branding
- Close-up of stitching and materials
- Size label and any tags

**Visual comparison**: Below the photos, you see the original product listing images for comparison. Examine the QC photos carefully — check color accuracy, logo placement, stitching quality, and overall construction.

**Decision interface**: Two buttons appear at the bottom:
- "Approve and Ship" — Click this if the item meets your expectations
- "Request Changes" — Click this if you notice issues

**If you approve**: Click "Approve and Ship." A confirmation dialog appears: "Item approved. Your order will proceed to packaging and shipping." Click "Confirm." The order status updates to "Approved — Preparing for Shipment."

**If you request changes**: Click "Request Changes." A form appears where you can specify the issue — wrong color, visible defect, size mismatch, etc. You can also request additional photos from different angles. Submit the form and the support team receives your request. They typically respond within 24 hours with updated photos or a replacement item.

**Visual confirmation**: Once approved, the order status changes to "Packaging." Within 1-2 business days, it updates to "Shipped" and you receive a tracking number.

For detailed guidance on interpreting QC photos and identifying quality issues, the [Kakobuy shipping guide](/Kakobuy-shipping-guide/) includes visual examples and best practices.

## Stage 5: Shipping, Tracking, and Delivery

**What you see**: In your dashboard, navigate to "Order History" and click on your order. The order detail page shows a tracking timeline:
- Order Placed (date)
- Payment Confirmed (date)
- QC Inspection (date)
- Approved for Shipping (date)
- Shipped (date)
- In Transit (current status)
- Delivered (estimated date)

**Tracking information**: Below the timeline, you see the tracking number and a link to the carrier's website. Click the link to view real-time tracking updates on the carrier's platform. You can also track directly within the Kakobuy dashboard — the tracking panel updates automatically as the carrier scans your package at each checkpoint.

**Visual tracking**: The tracking panel shows a map with the package's current location and a list of scan events:
- Departed facility in China
- Arrived at export hub
- In transit to destination country
- Cleared customs
- Arrived at local distribution center
- Out for delivery
- Delivered

**Delivery day**: The tracking status updates to "Delivered" with the date and time. Check your mailbox or porch for the package. Kakobuy uses discreet packaging — plain boxes or poly mailers with no external branding that would identify the contents.

**Unboxing**: Open the package and inspect the item. Compare it against the QC photos you approved. Everything should match. If there are any discrepancies or damage, contact support within 7 days through the dashboard ticket system.

**Visual confirmation**: In your dashboard, the order status updates to "Delivered — Complete." You can leave a review, reorder the same item, or browse for new products.

## FAQ

### Q1: How long does the complete Kakobuy walkthrough process take from start to finish?
A: The entire process from account creation to delivery typically takes 2-3 weeks. Account setup takes 5 minutes, product selection takes 15-30 minutes, checkout takes 5-10 minutes, QC review happens 2-3 days after payment, and delivery takes 10-14 days with standard shipping.

### Q2: What do the QC photos actually show?
A: QC photos show the actual item you will receive — not stock photos. You see multiple angles including front, back, sides, close-ups of logos and branding, stitching details, material texture, and size labels. These photos allow you to verify the item matches the listing before it ships.

### Q3: Can I track my package in real-time?
A: Yes, Kakobuy provides real-time tracking through the dashboard. Once your order ships, you receive a tracking number that updates automatically as the carrier scans your package at each checkpoint. You can view the tracking timeline and map directly in your dashboard or on the carrier's website.

### Q4: What if I am not satisfied with the QC photos?
A: If the QC photos reveal issues — wrong color, visible defects, or significant differences from the listing — you can request a replacement or additional photos. The support team responds within 24 hours. Resolution rates for legitimate claims exceed 95%.
