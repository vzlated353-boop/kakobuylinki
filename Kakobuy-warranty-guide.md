## Warranty Exclusions & Limitations

Understanding warranty exclusions prevents claim denials and helps customers maintain coverage validity. Kakobuy warranty policies include specific limitations that define coverage boundaries and customer responsibilities. Awareness of these terms supports proper product care and informed claim decisions.

Normal wear and tear exclusions apply across all categories. Electronics batteries naturally degrade over time with capacity below 70% within warranty periods qualifying, but gradual decline above this threshold represents normal use. Fashion items show natural material aging including slight color changes, softening of leather, and minor sole wear that don't qualify as defects. Accessories experience expected hardware wear including slight finish changes and minor loosening of adjustable components.

Water damage exclusions apply to non-waterproof electronics and fashion items. Items exposed to water beyond their rated resistance specifications void warranty coverage. Even water-resistant electronics face exclusions for saltwater exposure, high-pressure water contact, or water damage from opened housings. Customers should verify water resistance ratings before exposing items to moisture.

Unauthorized modification exclusions void warranty coverage when items are altered from original specifications. Electronics opened for repair by unauthorized service providers lose coverage. Fashion items altered by non-professional tailors face coverage limitations. Accessories modified with non-original components void hardware warranties. These exclusions protect warranty integrity while encouraging proper service channels.

Cosmetic damage exclusions apply to scratches, dents, and surface wear that don't affect functionality. Electronics with scratched screens that maintain full touch functionality don't qualify for warranty replacement. Fashion items with minor scuffs that don't compromise material integrity fall outside coverage. Accessories showing normal surface aging without structural impact remain excluded.

Time limit exclusions void coverage after warranty periods expire. Claims filed after the 30, 60, or 90-day coverage windows receive denial regardless of issue validity. Customers should monitor warranty expiration dates and report issues promptly. The platform's delivery confirmation system establishes warranty start dates for accurate tracking.
